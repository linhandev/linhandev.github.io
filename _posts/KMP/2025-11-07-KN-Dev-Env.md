## 安装依赖

- java：`brew install --cask temurin@21 zulu@8`
- xcode：
  - 从[https://xcodereleases.com](https://xcodereleases.com/)下载
  - 15.0 放到 /Applications/Xcode-15.0.app (仅2.0腾讯版本需要)
  - 16.0 放到 /Applications/Xcode-16.4.app
  - 通过 `sudo xcode-select -s /Applications/Xcode-16.4.app/Contents/Developer/` 或 `export DEVELOPER_DIR=/Applications/Xcode-16.4.app/Contents/Developer`
