# 逻辑回归

$$
z=w^{T}x+b  \\

\hat{y}=a=\sigma(z) \quad \sigma(x) = \frac{1}{1+e^{-x}} \\

Loss(a,y) = -( ylog(a) + (1-y)log(1-a) )
$$

反向从后往前求导
$$
\frac{dLoss(a,y)}{da} = -\frac{y}{a} + \frac{1-y}{1-a} \\

\sigma(x)= (1+e^{-x})^{-1} \\

\begin{aligned}
\frac{d\sigma(z)}{dz} &= -(1+e^{-z})^{-2}e^{-z}*-1 \\
&= e^{-z}*(1+e^{-z})^{-2} \\
&= (1+e^{-z}-1) * (1+e^{-z})^{-2} \\
&= \frac{1}{1+e^{-z}}(1-\frac{1}{1+e^{-z}}) \\
&= \sigma(z)(1-\sigma(z))
\end{aligned}
$$
$$
\begin{aligned}
\frac{dLoss(a,y)}{dz} &= \frac{dLoss(a,y)}{da} \frac{da}{dz} \\
&= (-\frac{y}{a} + \frac{1-y}{1-a}) * a(1-a) \\
&= a(1-y) + (a-1)*y \\
&= a-y
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\frac{dLoss(a,y)}{dw_{t}} = x_{t} * \frac{dLoss(a,y)}{dz}
$$
